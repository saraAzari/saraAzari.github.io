Beanfield Sales Application

My Role: UX research, usability tests, UI, interaction design, acting product owner.

Timeline: January 2021 - Present

The Problem

Context

Beanfield needed to upgrade its decade-old internal sales tool to accommodate company growth and changing processes.

User Needs

  • Clearer opportunity status tracking
  • Improved search functionality
  • Elimination of duplicate leads
  • Sales forecasting capabilities for leaders
  • Simplified information architecture and UI

Business Goals

Collaborated with the VP of Sales, CEO, COO, and sales leaders to streamline sales processes, eliminate manual work, and empower the sales team to generate more leads and close more deals.

The Solution

Design Process

Conducted user research, including interviews and observation, to understand sales reps' daily workflows, pain points, and goals. Created user personas to align the team on user needs. Developed wireframes and prototypes, iterating with stakeholders and developers. Defined the MVP scope and conducted usability testing to gather qualitative and quantitative data.

Key Features

New Design System

Developed "Hopscotch," a new design system for internal products, inspired by and built on Vuetify's implementation of Material Design.

Opportunity Page

Redesigned opportunity page provides a clear overview of each opportunity, including client details, estimated value, service categories, and status. Enables sales reps to add multiple quotes with different categories and addresses, each with various package and term offerings. Includes a communication card for tracking emails, comments, and forms. Facilitates follow-up tasks with due dates and notifications.

Search and Advanced Search

Implemented live search for clients, opportunities, activities, and parent companies. Advanced search allows for detailed queries, saved reports, and sharing capabilities.

Linked Accounts

Enabled linking of client accounts under a parent company for accurate revenue reporting.

Meeting Tracking

Integrated with Google Calendar to track client meetings, providing insights into sales progress.

Sales Dashboard

Designed a dashboard to display key performance indicators (KPIs) at a glance, including quota, open opportunities, forecast, meetings, and monthly revenue.

Pricing Browser

Provides access to available products based on client address, enabling accurate and efficient quoting.

Myquotes Page

Created a temporary public URL for sharing live quotes with clients, streamlining communication and reducing manual effort.

Design Decisions

Information Architecture

Prioritized search functionality with a prominent live search field on the dashboard. Utilized a left navigation drawer with two levels of entries, following the Hopscotch design system. Employed tabs to categorize related information and breadcrumbs for clear navigation and context.

Visual Design

Adhered to the Hopscotch design system for UI choices, ensuring accessibility and consistency. Created or modified components as needed.
